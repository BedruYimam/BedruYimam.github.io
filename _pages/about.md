# About Me

I am **Bedru Yimam**, a researcher and academic based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. My work focuses on bridging the gap between technology and social impact, with particular interest in data science applications for developing economies.

My research explores how computational methods can be leveraged to address challenges in education, healthcare, and sustainable development. I am passionate about open science, reproducible research, and building tools that make data analysis accessible to researchers across disciplines.
