Computer Science Education at Wollo University: Courses, Materials, and Student Supervision

## Teaching Philosophy My teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging, inclusive, and practical learning environment where students develop both theoretical understanding and practical skills. I believe in: - **Active Learning:** Engaging students through hands-on projects, discussions, and problem-solving activities - **Real-World Relevance:** Connecting course material to current industry practices and research challenges - **Inclusive Pedagogy:** Creating an accessible learning environment for diverse student backgrounds - **Continuous Improvement:** Regularly updating course content based on student feedback and technological advancements - **Mentorship:** Guiding students beyond the classroom to support their academic and professional development ## Current Courses (2023-2024 Academic Year) ### Undergraduate Courses
